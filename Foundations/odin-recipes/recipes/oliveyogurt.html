Olive Yogurt

Description

Very good recipe that my mom used to make me when I was a child

Ingredients

  • 1 little Danone yogurt
  • a bunch of olives to taste

Steps

  1. Get a spoon so you will be able to taste it properly
  2. Open up the little Danone
  3. Throw all olives inside the yogurt
  4. Mix it well
  5. Enjoy
